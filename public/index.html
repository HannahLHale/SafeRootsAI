    🔐 Cybersecurity Essentials

    🔒 Use Strong Passwords

    Avoid common phrases and include uppercase, numbers, and symbols.

    📧 Beware of Phishing

    Don’t click on suspicious links or share personal info via email.

    🛡️ Enable Two-Factor Authentication

    Double your protection by adding a second verification step.

    🖥️ Keep Software Updated

    Install updates regularly to patch security vulnerabilities.

    📱Don't Post Personal Information Online

    Avoid posting any content online that reveals any of your personal information.

    🗄️Create Backups

    Backup data on your devices frequently.
